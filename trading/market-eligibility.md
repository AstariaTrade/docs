---
title: Market Eligibility
---

Not every prediction market is suitable for an Astaria perpetual.

Astaria applies eligibility checks before listing an event-linked perp market.

## Quantitative Suitability

A market may be evaluated based on observable characteristics such as:

- Available liquidity
- Depth near the midpoint
- Trading activity
- Price stability
- Resolution timing
- Expected jump risk

## Qualitative Suitability

Astaria may also assess qualitative risks, including:

- Ambiguous resolution wording
- Oracle uncertainty
- Dispute history
- Cross-market manipulation concerns
- Event integrity risks

## Listing Decision

A market may be:

- Approved for listing
- Approved with tighter margin and lower leverage
- Deferred pending more liquidity or clarity
- Rejected if the event is unsuitable for perpetual trading

Market eligibility exists to ensure that leverage is only offered where the underlying event structure can support it.
